5 Delicious Steak Dishes

  1. Filet Mignon

    A tender cut from the beef tenderloin, often considered one of the most tender steaks. It's typically cooked to medium-rare or medium to preserve its tenderness and flavor.

  2. Ribeye Steak

    Known for its rich marbling, the ribeye is flavorful and juicy. It's often grilled or pan-seared to bring out its natural richness.

  3. New York Strip Steak

    A well-marbled and flavorful steak cut from the short loin. It's great for grilling and pan-searing, offering a good balance of tenderness and taste.

  4. T-Bone Steak

    Named for the T-shaped bone that separates the New York strip and the tenderloin. It's a combination of two flavorful cuts and is excellent for grilling.

  5. Sirloin Steak

    A versatile and affordable cut with a good balance of tenderness and flavor. It can be grilled, pan-seared, or broiled to perfection.
