5 Delicious Continental Chicken Dishes

  1. Chicken Alfredo Pasta

    A classic Italian-American dish featuring fettuccine pasta tossed in a creamy Alfredo sauce with grilled chicken.

  2. Coq au Vin

    A French dish of chicken braised with red wine, mushrooms, and bacon, resulting in a rich and flavorful stew.

  3. Chicken Cordon Bleu

    A dish where chicken breasts are stuffed with ham and Swiss cheese, then breaded and baked or fried until golden brown.

  4. Lemon Garlic Roast Chicken

    A roasted chicken infused with the flavors of lemon, garlic, and herbs, creating a deliciously savory and aromatic dish.

  5. Chicken Piccata

    An Italian dish featuring pan-seared chicken in a lemon and caper sauce, providing a delightful combination of tangy and savory flavors.
