5 Delicious Chinese Dishes

  1. General Tso's Chicken

    A popular dish featuring deep-fried chicken pieces coated in a sweet and slightly spicy sauce.

  2. Dim Sum

    A variety of bite-sized dishes traditionally served in small steamer baskets or on small plates, including dumplings, buns, and rolls.

  3. Peking Duck

    A famous dish with crispy duck skin served with thin pancakes, hoisin sauce, and sliced scallions.

  4. Hot Pot

    A communal cooking method where diners cook a variety of meats, vegetables, and noodles in a simmering pot of broth at the table.

  5. Kung Pao Chicken

    A flavorful and slightly spicy stir-fry dish with chicken, peanuts, vegetables, and chili peppers.
