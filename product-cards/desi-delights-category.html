5 Delicious Desi Delights

  1. Biryani

    A flavorful rice dish cooked with aromatic spices, basmati rice, and marinated meat (chicken, mutton, or beef).

  2. Paneer Tikka

    Chunks of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) marinated in a spiced yogurt mixture and grilled or baked to perfection.

  3. Butter Chicken

    A creamy and rich curry made with tender pieces of chicken in a tomato and butter-based sauce, often served with naan or rice.

  4. Samosas

    Deep-fried pastries filled with a mixture of spiced potatoes, peas, and sometimes meat, making for a popular snack or appetizer.

  5. Chana Masala

    A vegetarian dish made with chickpeas cooked in a flavorful blend of spices, tomatoes, and onions.
